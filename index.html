HENGAN

Pay As You Feel Restaurant by Henry and Megan

We provide a space where anyone can get access to nutritious food and learn more about food waste and sustainability

Who we are:

We are a couple that has always been conscious about the food waste problem in society and we wanted to create a platform/place where we can combat this problem by educating anyone who is interested.

Why we do it:

We want to do this because we believe food waste is one of the biggest problems for unsustainability in society and if we can try make our eating habits more eco-friendly we can help save the planet and the people on it at the same time.

Monthly delivery:

We provide monthly deliveries of fresh, nutritional produce and meals to anyone on a pay as you feel concept. If you can't afford it you can also volunteer time as payment. We accept all donations of food.

Education:

We want to educate people on the problem of food waste and how “wasted food” can be turned into nutritional rich and delicious meals.
